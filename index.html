1. Overview

Coinsera (“the App”) is a finance tracker designed to help users manage their personal savings, income, and expenses. Your privacy is extremely important to us.

2. No Data Collection

Coinsera does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or financial information to our own external servers. All data you enter into the app is saved locally on your device only.

3. Smart Features & Internet Usage

The app works primarily offline. However, three optional features require internet access to function:

  • Receipt Scanning: Images captured are sent securely to OCR.space for text recognition.
  • Voice Input: Audio recorded is sent securely to Groq/OpenAI for transcription.
  • Currency Rates: Requests are sent to ExchangeRate-API (open.er-api.com) to fetch current rates. No personal data is transmitted.

These files/requests are processed instantly and are not stored permanently by Coinsera.

4. Data Storage

All user data — including accounts, transactions, and preferences — are stored locally on your device’s storage. If you uninstall the app or clear its data, all stored information will be permanently deleted.

5. Permissions

Coinsera may request permissions for:

  • Storage: For exporting CSV files.
  • Camera: For scanning receipts.
  • Microphone: For voice commands.

6. Security

Because all information is stored locally on your device, protecting your device with a passcode or encryption is recommended to ensure your financial data remains private.

7. Contact

If you have any questions, please contact:

1. Dashboard & Net Worth

The Dashboard gives you a snapshot of your financial health. By default, it shows your Total Savings. You can toggle "Include Credit" or "Include Assets" in Settings to see a comprehensive Net Worth calculation.

  • Tap legend items on the Pie Chart to filter the view.
  • Tap the "Net Worth" card text to see details.

2. Reports & Trends

Navigate to the "Reports" tab to visualize your spending and income over time.

  • Trends: Switch between 6-Month and 1-Year views to see your financial trajectory.
  • History: Use the arrow buttons to change the selected month. The list below shows every transaction for that period.

3. Spending Heatmap

The Heatmap (found in the Reports tab) visualizes your spending intensity for the month.

  • Relative Intensity: The app finds your highest spending day of the month and sets that as "100%" (Deep Red). All other days are colored relative to that benchmark.
  • Green (Zero): No money left your accounts that day.
  • Red (High): You spent a significant amount compared to your usual habits.

4. Accounts & Savings

Track your liquid assets here. You can add bank accounts or e-wallets (GCash, Maya).

  • Interest: Set an interest rate (e.g., 4% p.a.) and a cycle (Monthly/Daily). Coinsera automatically calculates gains and deducts the 20% withholding tax.

5. Settings & Data

Manage your app preferences and personal data.

  • Profile: Tap the pencil icon next to your username to edit it. Tap the checkmark to save.
  • Export: Tap "Export All Transactions" to generate a CSV file of your entire financial history for use in Excel or Google Sheets.

6. Physical Assets

Track items that have value but aren't cash, such as Real Estate, Vehicles, or Gold. These contribute to your Net Worth.

  • Updates to asset details (like Name/Notes) are logged as system events.
  • Updates to the asset's Value are logged as financial corrections.

7. Credit & Loans

Manage your liabilities in the "Credit & Loan" tab.

  • Credit Cards: Track your used balance vs. credit limit. Set a Monthly Interest Rate (MIR) to auto-calculate charges.
  • Loans: Track installment payments. The loan closes automatically when fully paid.

8. Smart Transactions

Use AI to speed up your tracking:

  • Scan Receipt: Tap the Camera icon to capture a receipt. The app uses OCR to find the total amount.
  • Voice Input: Tap the Mic icon and say something like "Spent 500 on Food". The app will fill in the details for you.

* These features require an active internet connection.

9. Currency Converter

Quickly check international exchange rates against the Philippine Peso (PHP) or other currencies.
